My third Project

Oil painting tree of life

This was my third painting. I began working on it in May 2018 and finished it in in August of the same year;

The picture of the Tree of Life is multicolored, each color has its own meaning and meaning. The tree is blooming, it brings joy, happiness and fruits. A tree of abundance and prosperity. Bright and positive picture will decorate the house and fill it with warmth and joy.

A person has 7 chakras and if they are developed and filled, his life proceeds easily and in an involuntary way. Man is in harmony with himself and the world. For a married couple it is a symbol of love, fertility, prosperity, loyalty and happiness. Family is our foundation, our family and our home is our fortress.

My Fourth Project

Rhapsody in Green and Gold

This vibrant post-impressionist masterpiece was born from a fevered dream during the artist's stay at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum. Struggling with inner demons, the painter found solace in the asylum's surrounding landscape;

This particular view, with its undulating fields and dramatic sky, represented a moment of pure joy and connection with the natural world. The exaggerated brushstrokes and vivid colors reflect the artist's emotional state, transforming a simple rural scene into a celebration of life and resilience..;

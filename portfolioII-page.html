My Second Project

The Green Hills

This was my second painting. I began working on it in april 2019 and finished it in in august of the same year;

It was inspired by one of my visits to the The Altai mountains of Altai region, these mountains are a real natural and preserved gem in the heart of the Russian territory. Rivers, lakes, wildlife and flowers offer stunning landscapes that definitely worth the hike.

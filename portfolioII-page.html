My Second Project

The Green Hills

This was my second painting. I began working on it in January 2016 and finished it in March of the same year.

It was inspired by one of my visits to the scenic fields of Kiambu County, on the northern side of the Aberdare slopes.

On one sunny morning, the fields were very clear, with flowers spanning miles unending. The scenery was beckoning, urging me to pick up my brush and recreate it.

My Third Project

The Serene Portrait

This painting, titled The Serene Portrait, is a reflection of my artistic journey, created in early 2023. It showcases the beauty and depth of human emotion, capturing the subtleties of everyday life.

The inspiration for this piece came from observing people in various situations, where each expression tells a unique story. Through careful choice of colors and brush strokes, I aimed to encapsulate the essence of the moment, inviting viewers to pause and connect with their own emotions.
